Vega Chemicals Pvt. Ltd.

Vega Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Company. It was established in 1990 manufacturing, exporting & marketing of CALX Synthetic Red, Yellow & Orange Iron Oxide Pigments. Our plants are situated in Jalgaon & Bhusawal (M.S.) with production capacity of around 650 metric tons per month. Our corporate office is located at M.I.D.C. Jalgaon (M.S.) India. Both cities are well connected by road and rail network.
